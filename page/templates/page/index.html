Get Started

Whether you're a Customer looking to receive top-notch services or an Artisan ready to share your skills, getting started with TYSBE booking is simple!

For Customers
  1. Download and Register

    2. Install the app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) and sign up to create your account as a customer.

  2. Explore Services

    3. Browse our wide range of services, from mobile massage and braiding to 24/7 towing and car washing.

  3. Book and Pay

    4. Schedule a service at your convenience, confirm the details, wait for an artisan to accept and pay securely using Stripe.

  4. Receive and Review

    5. Enjoy the service at your location and leave a review to share your experience.

For Artisans
  1. Download and Sign Up

    2. Install the app and create an Artisan account by providing details about your skills and services.

  2. Set Availability

    3. Update your profile with your work schedule and service areas to attract potential customers.

  3. Accept Booking

    4. Receive service requests, confirm bookings, and provide exceptional services to customers.

  4. Get Paid

    5. Receive secure payments directly to your bank account after completing each service.
